Mission

  1. BUMA is an organization focused on giving our members the skills and experience they need to be successful in the marketing world.
  2. We pair students with local businesses to work with them on their marketing needs. We also travel to conferences and hold mini lessons on different marketing tactics.
  3. Just this past winter break we held an alumni dinner in the city to help our members network with marketing professionals.
  4. BUMA is great for marketing and non-marketing majors looking into a career path in marketing!

Story

  1. BU Marketing Association is an outlet for students of all majors to become acquainted with marketing professions.
  2. Marketing may be a major only offered in the School of Management, however there are countless opportunities and possibilities of marketing careers for those with any major!
  3. We work to gain real world marketing experience by working with various organizations on campus and within the Binghamton Community.
  4. Come learn more about what we do by attending our meetings and events!
